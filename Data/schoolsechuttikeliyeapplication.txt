Here is a sample application for leave from school:

**Leave Application for [Child's Name]**

**To,**

The Principal,
[School Name]
[School Address]

**Date,**

Subject: Request for Leave for [Number] Days

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am writing to request a leave for my child, [Child's Name], from [School Name] for [Number] days starting from [Date] to [Date]. The reason for the leave is [briefly mention the reason, e.g., family trip, personal work, health issue, etc.].

I have made necessary arrangements for my child's education during this period and will ensure that all his/her schoolwork is completed before the leave. I will also provide any necessary documents or assignments to the school before my child's departure.

I would be grateful if you could grant me this leave. If there are any issues or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

**Parents' Signature:** ______________________________________
**Child's Name:** ________________________________________
**Class:** ________________________________________
**Date of Birth:** ______________________________________

**Note:** Please attach a photocopy of the child's ID card and a medical certificate (if applicable) with the application.
