Here are a few more sample letters for university assignments:

**Request for Extension**

Dear [Tutor's Name],

I am writing to request an extension on the upcoming assignment in [Course Name] due to [briefly explain the reason for the request, e.g. "unforeseen personal circumstances" or "technical issues with my computer"]. I am confident that with a short extension, I will be able to complete the assignment to the best of my ability and meet the expected standards.

The original deadline for the assignment is [Original Deadline], and I am respectfully requesting an extension of [Specific Number of Days] to submit the assignment. I am available to discuss this request further and provide any necessary documentation to support my request.

Thank you for considering my request.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

**Request for Clarification**

Dear [Tutor's Name],

I am writing to request clarification on the instructions for the upcoming assignment in [Course Name]. Specifically, I am unsure about [specific aspect of the assignment, e.g. "the weighting of the different components" or "the expected format of the submission"]. I would greatly appreciate any guidance or clarification you can provide to help me complete the assignment successfully.

If possible, could you please provide a brief explanation or example to help me understand the requirements better? I am confident that with your clarification, I will be able to complete the assignment to the best of my ability.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

**Notification of Late Submission**

Dear [Tutor's Name],

I am writing to notify you that I will be submitting my assignment for [Course Name] late. Due to [briefly explain the reason for the delay, e.g. "unforeseen personal circumstances" or "technical issues with my computer"], I have been unable to complete the assignment on time. I am submitting the assignment as soon as possible and am confident that it meets the expected standards.

I would like to request your understanding and accommodation in this matter. Please let me know if there are any additional steps I need to take or if you require any documentation to support my late submission.

Thank you for your understanding.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
